Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built by songwriters who got tired of switching between five different apps.

🎭

Voice Profiles

During onboarding, the AI learns your writing style — your tendencies, your vocabulary, your instincts. Suggestions sound like you, not a machine.

🔀

Section Versioning

Fork any section to try a different direction. Compare A/B versions side by side. Keep what works, discard what doesn't — safely.

📋

Scratch Pad

Collect AI suggestions, stray ideas, and half-formed lines without leaving your flow. Drag them into your lyrics when you're ready.

🎯

Rhyme Browser

Filter rhymes by syllable count, perfect vs. near matches, and more. A proper rhyme dictionary, not a toy.

⚡

Intensifiers Panel

Mood-aware power words at your fingertips. When a line needs more weight, browse words that match the emotional register you're working in.

🎙️

Audio Recorder

Capture melody ideas, vocal phrasing, or rough demos right alongside your lyrics. Everything stays in one place.

📖

Full Song Overview

See your entire song in a split view. Jump between sections, spot structural issues, and get the big picture without losing your place.

⏪

History & Undo

Experiment without fear. Full history lets you rewind to any previous state. Try things — you can always go back.

🌐

Bilingual EN/FR

Full interface and AI tools available in both English and French. Write in the language that fits the song.
