# 20 Essential Songwriting Resources
*Compiled for Jac Gautreau — March 2026*

---

## 📚 BOOKS

1. **Songwriters on Songwriting** — Paul Zollo
   52 in-depth interviews with legendary writers: Dylan, Simon, Mitchell, Newman, Seeger, Zappa, Madonna, R.E.M., Tom Petty, and more.
   https://archive.org/details/songwritersonson0000zoll

2. **Writing Better Lyrics** — Pat Pattison (Berklee)
   The definitive academic guide to lyric writing: prosody, object writing, song structure.
   https://archive.org/search?query=writing+better+lyrics+pattison

3. **Songwriting: Essential Guide to Lyric Form and Structure** — Pat Pattison
   Deep dive into AABA, verse-chorus, prosody, and the architecture of a great song.
   https://www.amazon.com/Songwriting-Essential-Structure-Techniques-Writing/dp/0793511801

4. **How Music Works** — David Byrne (Talking Heads)
   A musician's philosophical + practical meditation on how songs connect with listeners.
   https://archive.org/details/howmusicworks0000byrn_c8i0

5. **Finishing the Hat** — Stephen Sondheim
   Collected lyrics 1954–1981 with Sondheim's own commentary on craft, rhyme, and storytelling.
   https://archive.org/details/finishinghat00

6. **Songwriting Without Boundaries** — Pat Pattison
   Object writing exercises that build sensory specificity — the #1 skill separating good lyrics from great ones.
   https://www.amazon.com/Songwriting-Without-Boundaries-Writing-Exercises/dp/1599632977

7. **The Addiction Formula** — Friedemann Findeisen (Holistic Songwriting)
   Data-driven breakdown of what makes songs emotionally addictive: tension, release, contrast, surprise.
   https://www.holistic-songwriting.com/about

## 🎓 FREE ONLINE COURSES & CURRICULA

8. **Introduction to Songwriting** — Coursera / Berklee (Pat Pattison)
   Free audit available: covers prosody, lyric form, melody, and rhyme across 5 weeks.
   https://www.coursera.org/learn/songwriting

9. **Berklee Online Songwriting Degree Handbook** — Berklee Press (Free PDF)
   Full curriculum overview of how Berklee structures songwriting education.
   https://strangefangs.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/Berklee-Online-Songwriting-Degree-Handbook.pdf

10. **The Essential Secrets of Songwriting** — Gary Ewer
    Hundreds of free articles on every aspect of craft: melody, chords, lyric, structure, hooks.
    https://www.secretsofsongwriting.com/

## 🎙️ PODCASTS

11. **Sodajerker On Songwriting**
    In-depth interviews with world-class songwriters on craft, process, and inspiration.
    Best episodes: McCartney, Dolly Parton, Carole King, Nick Cave, Nile Rodgers.
    https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/sodajerker-on-songwriting/id479679002

12. **And The Writer Is… with Ross Golan**
    Hollywood songwriter interviews major hitmakers (Ryan Tedder, Diplo, Jon Bellion, etc.) about craft.
    https://www.andthewriteris.com

13. **Songcraft: Spotlight on Songwriters**
    Career-spanning interviews with songwriters across genres and generations.
    https://www.songcraftshow.com/

## 📺 YOUTUBE

14. **Holistic Songwriting** — Friedemann Findeisen
    Analytical breakdowns of what makes major artists emotionally powerful (14M+ views).
    https://www.youtube.com/@HolisticSongwriting

15. **Rick Beato — "What Makes This Song Great?"** playlist
    Deconstructs hit songs stem-by-stem, showing exactly how melody, harmony, and production work together.

16. **Mary Spender**
    UK singer-songwriter covering craft, inspiration, and the practical reality of writing.
    https://www.youtube.com/@MarySpender

## 📰 LONG-FORM ESSAYS & ARTICLES

17. **The Rolling Stone Interview: Bob Dylan (20th Anniversary)**
    Dylan's rare, extended discussion of songwriting process and philosophy.
    https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/the-rolling-stone-20th-anniversary-interview-bob-dylan-194911/

18. **Joni Mitchell Library — Rolling Stone Interview, 1979**
    Mitchell discusses her compositional methods, tunings, influences, and creative philosophy.
    https://jonimitchell.com/library/view.cfm?id=300

19. **Andrea Stolpe — Songwriting Articles**
    Former Berklee instructor and staff writer on the craft of professional lyric writing.
    https://www.andreastolpe.com/articles

20. **Cognition, Interaction, and Creativity in Songwriting Sessions** — SAGE (2025)
    Academic paper exploring how cognition and collaboration work in professional songwriting.
    https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/01902725241266138
